Frequently asked questions

Does it run on Apple Silicon?

Yes. The binary is universal: it runs natively on Apple Silicon (M1 and later) and on Intel Macs. Requires macOS 14 Sonoma or later.

Is it free?

Yes, completely free.

Do I need to create an account?

No. The app uses the sessions you're already signed in to in Claude Code (claude) and/or Codex (codex). If you don't use those CLIs, the app has nothing to show you.

What about my privacy?

Everything happens on your Mac. The app reads your CLIs' local credentials and queries only Anthropic's and OpenAI's official usage endpoints. No analytics, no tracking, no developer servers in between.

Is it safe to install?

Yes: it's signed with a Developer ID and notarized by Apple, which means Apple scanned the binary. It opens with a normal double-click.

How do I get updates?

The app itself lets you know when a new version is available and takes you to the download. You can also check for updates manually in Settings.

Is this an official Anthropic or OpenAI app?

No. It's an independent tool made by a solo developer; Claude and Codex are trademarks of their respective companies.
